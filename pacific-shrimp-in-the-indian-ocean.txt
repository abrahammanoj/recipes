Ingredients:
 - 2 cups of pacific cooked shrimp small frozen
 - 1 teaspoon salt
 - 2 teaspoons nirapara meat masala
 - 2 eggs
 - 1/4 cup of peanut oil
 - 1 tomato cut into small pieces
 - 2 thai chilli peppers chopped into small pieces

Directions:
 1. Defreeze the shrimp by putting it in a bowl of cold water. Drain and dry the
 shrimp in a paper towel.

 2. Heat the peanut oil in a pan over medium heat.

 3. Add the shrimp to the pan and saute it for 5 minutes.

 4. Add the thai chilli peppers, salt, masala and tomatoes and give it a stir.

 5. Beat the eggs and add it to the pan.

 6. Let it cook for another 5 minutes till it has the consistency like an
 omlette or fritata.
